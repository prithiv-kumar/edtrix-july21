What is Ideate?

Discover your aptitude

Intelligence is inadequate with ignorance of applying it in life. Aptitude is the inborn potential to apply our intelligence on all things. Ability is the knowledge and skills that one develop by constant learning and practice. Discovering your aptitude & ability helps you in realizing your potential. Knowing our potential can make us understand where do we stand and how to develop ourselves more and in which direction


Identify your passion

"I have no special talents. I am only passionately curious."

Albert Einstein

Passion makes you believe in doing things that your heart desire to. Identifying your passion can be narrowed down to your field of interests. Your field of interests makes a way for living, working and in decision making.


Make a career in what you love!

"Choose a job you love and you will never have to work a day in your life."

Confucius

Career is a major part of our life. Being happy at your career and loving what you do is an overall productivity booster and enhances performance. People who enjoy their jobs are more likely to be optimistic, motivated, learn faster, make fewer mistakes, and better business decisions.
