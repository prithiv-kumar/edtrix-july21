Objectives

  • Little Whizzarts sets a perfect platform and room for the children at this age to explore and learn things by their own with a little assistance through arts & crafts.
  • Artistic activities help children learn other subjects such as reading and math.
  • Art promotes visual, motor and social development
  • Art is an enriching communicating tool for children
  • Art allows children to externalize their feelings

Features

  • The program has been carefully designed to bring out the curiosity, develop the creativity and let them be imaginative, spontaneous and expressive.
  • The syllabus is evenly spread to 10 levels based on its complexity. Each student can go through each level on their own pace and move on the next level.
  • Each level explores the kid’s sketching, painting, Materials exploration, Model making skills.
