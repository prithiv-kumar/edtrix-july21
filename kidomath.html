Objectives

  • Knowledge - Improves the mathematical knowledge with conceptual thinking about the subject
  • Development - Students will be introduced to various forms of learning and relevant concepts.
  • Time Management - As the difficulties increases in the learning process they would introduced to various method of time management for every problem.
  • Academics enhancement - Kid-o-math curriculum make the students understand the nuances of mathematics which in turn results in the higher marks in their academics.
  • Problem solving – Problem-solving is important for the kids because it enables them to exert control over our environment.

Features

  • Kid-O-Math aims to change the whole perspective of math not being scary or difficult to approach/solve by breaking the math into simple basic structures.
  • Kid-O-Math makes mathematics as fun filled subjects with puzzles and activities in 10 levels, using the major pillars of math i.e. Quantity, space, Structure, Change, Reasoning and Quantitative.
