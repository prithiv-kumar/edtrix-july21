HACK-LAB

For Ages -6 to 17 year olds

Hack-Lab is a space where the students can experiment, explore and learn by getting their hands messy. Set up HACK-LAB in your schools to provide an immersive learning experience for your students by means of various workshops across disciplines such as Art, Design, Science and Technology, Mathematics, Social Sciences and Communication from the experts in those fields.

IDEATE

For Ages -15 to 18 years

IDEATE is an exclusive career awareness program that helps in discovering one’s potential, understanding their strengths and weaknesses and offers creative solutions to their future. With the NEP 2020 introducing stream selection to 8th standard students, help them make better decisions by organizing career awareness drives offered by IDEATE.

The IDEATE Process -

  • Multi-level Analysis & Psychometric tests which understands one’s basic learning processes, aptitude and skill sets. Along with tests, the students will also have a hands-on creative activity/exercise to completely evaluate their level of development under various criteria.
  • Detailed report of the student’s complete profile including their strengths, weaknesses, area of interest, aptitude, etc.
  • One-on-one or group career counselling from Educational & Industrial experts.

Benefits of joining IDEATE -

  • The students receive a complete report which will allow them to better understand themselves.
  • Easing the decision making process along with an awareness of niche and lucrative career paths.
  • Complete guidance from talking to industry experts, till getting into the University of their choice.
