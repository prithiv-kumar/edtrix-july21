Graphics Design Certification Program

Explore everything graphic design can offer to visualize and interpret content for the digital world.

Learn to manipulate images and to use a visual language for storytelling as well as for branding, print media and publications.

The major constituents of Graphic Design from a broader perspective include -

  • Graphical Imagery
  • Typographical Words
This course exposes the students to the basics & advanced manipulation techniques of graphic & textual elements along with a thorough hands-on practise of the key fundamentals of graphic design.

Why this particular Graphic Design Course?

To be a great graphic Designer it is not enough just to be aware of the tools that are available. During the course of the three months, along with getting fluent with using the various softwares and tools, you are trained to completely understand the processes of a Graphic Designer from ideation to creation.

From appreciating the various ways Graphic design has influenced the world and how history was always swayed by the designers, learn the rich history of graphic design. This enables you to make better design choices and truly experience the potentials of the graphical world.

Learn to weave magical stories and communicate through images and typography. Be it for your social media, blog, personal branding or for kick-starting your own graphic design career.

This certification course will set you up for a formal education in graphic design and also will act as a starting point for further work in interface design, motion graphics, animation and editorial design.
